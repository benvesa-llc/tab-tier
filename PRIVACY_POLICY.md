# Privacy Policy for Tab Tier

**Last updated:** April 13, 2026

## Data Collection

Tab Tier does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

## Data Storage

All data (tab information, user settings, tier assignments) is stored locally on your device using Chrome's storage API. No data is sent to external servers.

## Permissions

- **tabs:** Required to manage and monitor browser tabs
- **storage:** Required to save your settings and tab tier data locally
- **alarms:** Required for scheduled tab lifecycle operations

## Third-Party Services

Tab Tier does not use any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising services.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted in this file and reflected in the "Last updated" date above.
